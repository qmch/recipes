Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of orange juice (use prepared frozen juice not Tang)
 - brown sugar 1-2 tbsp approx.
 - 2-3 tbsp approx. prepared mustard (optional)
 - 1 can of Spam

Preparation:
1. Prepare sauce by mixing mustard (optional), brown sugar, and orange juice together.
2. Cut Spam into ⅛ to ¼" thick slices.
3. Fry slices in frying pan over medium heat until lightly browned on both sides.
4. Pour sauce over cooked Spam and simmer in pan for 10 minutes or until sauce thickens.
